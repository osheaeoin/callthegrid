Call The Grid
The Basics

Call The Grid is a free F1 prediction game. Before each race weekend, predict who you think will finish where — earn points based on accuracy. Compete against friends across a full season. No money, just F1 knowledge and luck.

Yes, completely free. No subscription, no premium tier, no ads.

📍In Top 10, wrong position+5
🏎️Correct Pole Position+5
